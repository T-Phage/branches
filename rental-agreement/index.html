5. Rental Fees


Renter will pay to Company monthly rental fees for use of the Vehicle as follows:


a. The rental fees include the rent (plus all statutory charges) and charges for additional services (including limitations of liability) provided by Company for maintenance of the Vehicle.


b. Monthly charges shall be as follows depending on car model rented:

- Gh¢800 for hatchback

- Gh¢900 for sedan

- Gh¢1,000 for SUV


c. Monthly rental period shall be 30 days commencing from date of hire.


d. Payment may be made via cash, money order or cheque



6. Bookings and Cancellations


a. All bookings shall be made via solar taxi’s website ( www.solartaxi.co )


b. Cancellation of bookings or rentals can only be done by filing the cancellation form supplied by Company via email or at the offices of the Company at No.6 Naa Asia Rd, Laterbiokorshie, Accra.


c. Cancellations by Renter after vehicle has been booked will attract a fee of 10% of the cost of booking.


d. In the event of no-show of the Renter, 25% of the cost of booking shall be charged



7. Transfer and Return of the Vehicle


a. The Company agrees to transfer to the Renter the Vehicle together with all documents required for driving (Registration Certificate and the Agreement).


b. The Renter undertakes to return the Vehicle to the Company in the place, on the date and at the time stated in the Agreement, and the Vehicle must be equipped in the same way and have the same documents as at the time of transfer of the Vehicle to the Renter.


c. The agreed place of return of the Vehicle (together with keys and registration documents) is the office of the Company at No. 6 Naa Ashia Road Laterbiokorshie. In the event of return at a place other than the office of the Company, the Vehicle shall be deemed not returned and the Renter shall be responsible for additional costs of 10% of the stated monthly charge per day until such time as the Vehicle shall be in Company’s direct possession.


d. Should the returned Vehicle require a more thorough cleaning than normal, the renter shall pay the cost.



8. Liability of the Renter


a. Renter is fully liable to the Company for damage, theft and loss of the Vehicle and any of its parts to the extent not covered by an insurance policy.


b. The liability of the Renter does not extend to malfunctions of battery, transmission and clutch not caused by incorrect driving technique.


c. Where the Vehicle is involved in an accident or is otherwise damaged through the fault of the Renter, the Renter shall be liable to pay a penalty of 100% of the monthly rental charge for the downtime caused by temporary inoperability of the Vehicle resulting from the damage caused to the Vehicle due to the accident or other incident.


d. Downtime is calculated from the day of the accident or the day of damage caused till day when the Vehicle is back in operation.
